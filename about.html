Meet our Team

Our Photographers

Sara Whitmer

Sara grew up in Western Pennsylvania. During her freshman year she moved to the Buffalo/Niagara Falls area and graduated high school while living there. After high school Sara fell in love with photography and started to take pictures for family and friends and then got her first wedding to shoot. Since then she has continued taking pictures whenever she can and loves what she does.

Tina Whitmer

Tina is Sara's mother. She became part of the team when the first wedding was booked. She started out just as a photographer and became a integral part of the team. Tina grew up in Western Pennsylvania as well. She does this photography on the side but enjoys taking photos of her grandsons and all family members.
